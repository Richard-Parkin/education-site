Advanced Content Filtering Features

SurfProtect Quantum is designed to completely suit the filtering requirements of any school or academy. Providing Prevent Duty-compliant filtering out the box, SurfProtect Quantum is completely customisable, giving users the ability to choose exactly what's blocked and allowed by their filtering, incorporating a huge range of features and options, including the below and more:

• Intuitive Online Panel: Through an easily usable online panel, teachers and administrators can plan exactly how their school's filtering policy works - what categories to block, what sites to allow, search keywords to ban, and far more.

• Active Directory Integration: SurfProtect Quantum allows for complete Active Directory integration, which gives administrators even more control over their filtering, letting them assign customised policies for user groups (like teachers and students), computers and individuals.

• Cross-Network Protection: As a Cloud-based service, SurfProtect Quantum can easily be set up on a network, giving schools effective, network-level content filtering that works across devices and computers without any additional setup.

• Automatic Site Categorisation: When a user attempts to access a website (including HTTPS sites), SurfProtect Quantum automatically categorises the site, updating our database in real-time, ensuring a more accurate level of filtering across every school using SurfProtect Quantum.

• Powerful Analytics Suite: SurfProtect Quantum includes a powerful analytics suite, which gives users the ability to see exactly how their connections are being used at any given time, seeing what searches and sites users attempt to make/ visit, what classification is applied, and who made the search/ visited the site.

SurfProtect Quantum also includes a much wider range of features, with many more being worked on by our in-house team to give schools even more convenience with their content filtering - stay tuned for more information about the ongoing development!
