Award-Winning Technical Support

We know how frustrating it is to have to wait around on hold when you're experiencing a problem. Website Hosting and Email Services

We can provide your school with everything you need for your website (except for design) - head over to our Domain Checker to check and register your domain. We also provide high quality shared and dedicated webspace hosting, designed to give you as complete a range of choices as possible, while our DNS admin panel gives you complete control over your DNS settings.

Our email services are included with our hosting services, and are also available separately. Our PupilMail service can be useful for primary schools, giving children a simple and safe introduction to using email, while our StaffMail system lets staff keep an eye on how their students are using email.

3/4G Connectivity Backup

Our school Internet connections are designed to be fully reliable at all times, but our 3/4G backup option provides your school with another layer of resilience if anything goes wrong. You'll be able to access the Internet through the SIM card we provide, allowing access to vital sites in case of emergencies.

Every one of our SIM cards is provided with a static IP address, meaning that your school's website & email services can be automatically forwarded from your main connection if there's an emergency.
