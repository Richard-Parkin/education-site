Connectivity

Advanced Internet Connectivity for Education

Internet connections for schools are often strained, with video streaming, mobile devices/ tablets, online learning resources and general computer usage all taking up a huge, and ever-increasing, amount of bandwidth.

It's now more important than ever for your school to have an Internet connection which fully accommodates for your students' needs - that's where Exa Education step in.

Why Choose Internet Connectivity from Exa Education?

As a leading independent Internet Service Provider, we offer schools a comprehensive range of Internet connectivity options, all designed to be completely reliable and resilient. Running and managing our own network, we're able to ensure that you get end-to-end service quality.

We stand out from the competition in five key areas:

•Choice: We offer schools a complete range of connectivity options based on location, as seen below, while also providing a full range of additional services.

•Speed: Because we run and monitor our own network, we're always able to ensure that there's enough bandwidth available to give you the speed that you pay for.

•Reliability: Using multiple data centres across the country, we provide our customers with automatic failover to keep their services working at all times, along with 3/4G backup connectivity.

•Price: We charge significantly lower prices than most other ISPs, typically saving schools thousands of pounds on their Internet bills. Call us at 0345 145 1234 for an obligation-free quote.

•Support: If anything goes wrong with your Internet connection, our award-winning support team are on call through extended business hours, helping to quickly solve any issues.

DarkLight

Our latest internet option for schools, DarkLight is designed to be the last internet connection you’ll ever need to install.

DarkLight offers completely future proofed connections, with symmetrical speeds that can be upgraded without additional construction at any time, from 100 Mbps to 10 Gbps, all coming at a significantly lower cost than other ultrafast connections and backed up by a high Service Level Agreement.

Available in a number of gigabit cities, DarkLight can make a huge difference to schools.

Leased Lines

Ideal for schools requiring a high amount of bandwidth, leased lines provide schools with a completely uncontended connection not shared with anyone else.

We offer a range of leased line speeds, letting your school get the level of bandwidth that you require, all the way up to 10 Gbps.

Leased Lines are completely symmetrical, so uploads are just as fast as downloads, while our 99.99% SLA ensures total reliability.

FTTC

Fibre to the Cabinet Broadband connectivity delivers high speeds, with up to 76 Mbps downloads and 20 Mbps uploads available.

The actual Internet speeds that your school receives from your FTTC connection is determined by your distance from the cabinet you use - we'll ensure that you get the best speeds possible.

We also offer EoFTTC (Ethernet over Fibre to the Cabinet) connectivity, letting you receive guaranteed speeds and an improved Service Level Agreement.

ADSL

Providing download speeds up to 24 Mbps, ADSL is the most widely available broadband service. Speeds are dependent on your location, but we'll ensure that you always get the best speed available.
