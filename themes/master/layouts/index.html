Welcome to Exa Education

We're a leading independent Internet Service Provider for schools and academies across the UK, providing a full range of advanced Internet services to thousands of learning institutions. Since 2003, we've worked to offer schools market-leading, completely flexible service packages designed to improve performance, while often significantly cutting costs for our customers.

As an independent ISP, we're able to offer a huge range of services for our customers, creating completely bespoke Internet service packages including everything from effective Internet connectivity to content filtering, VoIP and far more. With every school having different service requirements, get in touch with our team to discuss your requirements at 0345 145 1234 or via the contact form below.
